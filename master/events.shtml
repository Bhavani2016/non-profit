Clothing Swap


Join us for one of our monthly Clothes Swap!


Trusted Clothes and The Kidney Foundation of Canada have partnered together again to organize a FREE CLOTHES SWAP!

Bring your gently used, clean clothes that you don’t want anymore and leave with new (to you) stuff!
The event is COMPLETLY FREE and aims to raise awareness about textile waste and how our clothes affect the environment.
You can swap menswear, womenswear, children’s clothes, jackets, shoes and accessories. Please invite your friends!
