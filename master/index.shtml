Trusted Clothes is an organization linking people, organizations and brands that are ethical, environmentally friendly and health conscious. We are a group of volunteers with a diverse background in manufacturing, marketing and global issues dedicated to promoting Ethical, Sustainable and Healthy fashion.

Sustainable

Be aware of the impact and abuses the clothing industry has on the world. Be informed about the successes from people actively trying to change this industry, from inside and out.

Ethical

Be educated about ethical, sustainable and healthy alternative brands, the economics of fashion and the key domains of this industry.

Healthy

Connect conscious consumers and retailers, brands and manufacturers who provide sustainable and healthy alternatives to mainstream fashion as well as drive affirmative action from the grassroots. Starting with you. Your shopping habits, living habits, clothing habits.
