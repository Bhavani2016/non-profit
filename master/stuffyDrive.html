85% of textiles - including stuffed animals - end up in our lanfills. But together we can make a change!

Join us in diverting textiles from landfills and helping your community by donating your *used*stuffed toys!

We're excited to partner with the Waterloo Outreach Program, who will passout the collected pre-loved stuffies to children of low-income families. Our Collection Drop Box hosted by our local downtown Kitchener neighbours, Central Fresh Market.
